💬 About Safe Taxi

This website promotes the safety and confidence of women and children when boarding a taxi to avoid harassment.

It includes a real-time map where passengers can indicate their location so that a verified driver can pick them up safely.

Our system combines real-time tracking, emergency alerts, audible alarms, and an AI chatbot trained to provide safety guidance.

✨ Features

  • Real-time location tracking for passengers and drivers.
  • Emergency button for immediate help.
  • Direct call buttons for taxi drivers and emergency services.
  • Audible alerts for urgent situations.
  • AI chatbot trained to answer safety-related questions.
  • Map showing exact location and route for safe pick-up.
  • First aid instructions in case of emergencies.
