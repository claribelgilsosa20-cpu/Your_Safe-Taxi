💬 About Safe Taxi

This is a website to promote the safety and confidence of women and children when boarding a taxi to avoid harassment. It includes a real-time map where passengers can indicate their location so that a verified driver can pick them up without any issue. Our goal is to restore safety, confidence, and well-being to many women and children.

We have a complete safety system with an emergency button, real-time location, audible alerts, and an AI chatbot designed to answer questions and guide users in any situation.
