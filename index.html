    How Safe Taxi Works

    • Users register and share their location with verified drivers.
    • Passengers can track the taxi in real-time on the map.
    • Emergency alerts can be triggered anytime if the passenger feels unsafe.
    • Users can schedule pickup times via the calendar.
    • AI chatbot provides tips and safety guidance for passengers.

    Benefits of Using Safe Taxi

    • Enhanced safety for women and children.
    • Real-time ride tracking for peace of mind.
    • Instant access to emergency help.
    • Ability to schedule rides in advance.
    • Quick contact with verified taxi drivers.
